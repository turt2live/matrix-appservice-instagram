Welcome

This bridge tracks Instagram accounts and posts media that is uploaded to Matrix rooms. To get started, invite @_instagram:t2bot.io to a private conversation and type !help

Usage

To follow an individual user, join their Instagram room. For example, #_instagram_turt2live:t2bot.io is turt2live's Instagram room.

If you'd like an Instagram user to post to your own room, invite the virtual user directly, such as @_instagram_turt2live:t2bot.io
